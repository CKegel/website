About 5939

RPI Ambulance operates a 2006 AEV Trauma Hawk XL Type II ambulance mounted on a Ford E-350 chassis. This vehicle was placed in service in the Spring of 2007. The county vehicle identifier is 5939, however the ambulance was formerly known as A-39 and continues to hold this call sign within the agency. 5939 is the fifth van ambulance operated by RPI Ambulance. The first was placed in service in 1983.
