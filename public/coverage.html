Request Coverage

Here are a few options for how RPI Ambulance can provide EMS coverage for your event.
If you have further questions please feel free to contact us.

  • Type I

    $10/Hour
  • Long Duration; Low Capacity
  • No Primary Care Facility
  • At least 1 EMT
  • First Response 59
  • Oxygen and Medical Supplies
  • Sign up

  • Type II

    $30/Hour
  • Larger Spectator Event
  • No Primary Care Facility
  • 1-3 EMTs on Field Crews
  • 5939 (Available for 911 Calls)

    $75/Hour
  • Large Spectator Events (5000+)
